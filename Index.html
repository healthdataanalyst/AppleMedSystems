Who We Are

About AppleMed

AppleMed Systems & Research Private Limited is a global health consulting and research firm committed to transforming healthcare through data-driven insights, innovative design, and practical implementation. We work with organizations and innovators who are serious about creating lasting change in healthcare delivery.

Vision

An equitable world where preventive and primary healthcare innovations improve lives globally.

Mission

To provide evidence-based, data-driven solutions that turn ideas into impactful programs and products, supporting scalable and sustainable healthcare innovation across systems.

Founded to bridge the gap between healthcare innovation and real-world impact, AppleMed operates at the intersection of research, data science, and implementation. We believe that transformative healthcare solutions require more than good ideas‚Äîthey need rigorous evaluation, iterative testing, strategic planning, and data-informed design.

With offices in Hyderabad and San Francisco, we bring global expertise to local healthcare challenges. Our dual presence enables us to apply international best practices while maintaining deep understanding of regional contexts, regulations, and healthcare systems.

Rajesh is a global public health leader with over 15 years of experience bridging data science, innovation, and implementation. He specializes in public health strategy, AI-powered analytics, and evidence-driven program design.

Throughout his career, Rajesh has guided organizations and innovators through the complete journey from concept to market‚Äîconducting feasibility studies, developing prototypes, leading pilot implementations, and supporting scaled deployment. His work spans digital health platforms, community health interventions, and data systems strengthening across multiple countries.

Rajesh combines rigorous research methodology with system-level strategic thinking and human-centered design to create solutions that are both innovative and practical. He approaches every project with a focus on sustainability, equity, and measurable health outcomes.
