AppleMed

Rajesh Sirigireddy, MSHI

Executive Public Health Consultant | Digital Health & AI Strategist

Rajesh is a global public health leader with over 13 years of experience bridging data science, innovation, and implementation. He specializes in public health strategy, AI-powered analytics, and evidence-driven program design.

Throughout his career, Rajesh has guided organizations and innovators through the complete journey from concept to market—conducting feasibility studies, developing prototypes, leading pilot implementations, and supporting scaled deployment. His work spans digital health platforms, community health interventions, and data systems strengthening across multiple countries.

Rajesh combines rigorous research methodology with system-level strategic thinking and human-centered design to create solutions that are both innovative and practical. He approaches every project with a focus on sustainability, equity, and measurable health outcomes.
